Unit Circle and trigonometric functions

Using Right Triangles to Evaluate Trigonometric Functions

  • sin t = opposite / hypotenuse
  • cos t = adjacent / hypotenuse
  • tan t = opposite / adjacent
  • SohCahToa mnemonic

Reciprocal Trigonometric Functions

  • sec t = hypotenuse / adjacent
  • csc t = hypotenuse / opposite
  • cot t = adjacent / opposite
  • Reciprocal relationships: sin t = 1/csc t, cos t = 1/sec t, tan t = 1/cot t

Finding Trigonometric Functions of Special Angles Using Side Lengths

  • 30-60-90 triangle side ratios: s, √3s, 2s
  • 45-45-90 triangle side ratios: s, s, √2s
  • All six functions from side lengths: sin, cos, tan, sec, csc, cot

Using Equal Cofunctions of Complements

  • cos t = sin(π/2 − t) and sin t = cos(π/2 − t)
  • tan t = cot(π/2 − t) and cot t = tan(π/2 − t)
  • sec t = csc(π/2 − t) and csc t = sec(π/2 − t)
